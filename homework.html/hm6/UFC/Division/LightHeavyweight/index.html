Light Heavyweight Division

The light heavyweight division in mixed martial arts contains different weight classes.

  • The UFC's light heavyweight ranges from 186 to 205 lb (84 to 93 kg).
  • The ONE Championship's light heavyweight division (also known as cruiserweight) has an upper limit of 102.1 kg (225.1 lb).
  • The Road FC's light heavyweight division places the upper limit at 205 lb (93 kg).
Light heavyweight is a weight class in mixed martial arts, which generally refers to competitors weighing from 186 to 205 lb (84 to 93 kg).
It sits between the lighter middleweight division, and the heavyweight division. The light heavyweight limit,
as defined by the Nevada State Athletic Commission and the Association of Boxing Commissions is 206 lb (93 kg).
